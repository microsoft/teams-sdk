# Activity Handlers

A BotBuilder `ActivityHandler` works much like the Teams SDK `App` activity routing.
The `BotBuilderPlugin` accepts a BotBuilder `ActivityHandler` instance, 
letting you keep your existing handlers in place while moving any part you want over to the new Teams SDK handlers.
This enables a more gradual, incremental migration approach.
